We're Firley

February 15, 2024

Technology has always shaped how we solve problems, and right now, it’s taking a big leap. With long-context, multimodal language models, we’re finally able to tackle problems as messy and complex as the real world itself.

If we get this right, it won’t just make things more efficient; it’ll fundamentally change how we think about sustainability.

I do acknowledge that the chances of what we’re doing actually working out on a large scale is insanely low, and that’s why it’s a good thing that a lot of people are working hard to solve this problem and make life better for everyone. We hope that we can be a part of that too, and through tech, we can achieve just that. Making your life better.

I’m Omkar. I started Firley because I’ve always believed technology has the power to solve problems that feel too big or too messy to handle. Growing up, I saw the disconnect between what people wanted to do for the environment and what they had the tools to actually accomplish. With Firley, I want to bridge that gap: giving people smarter, more intuitive ways to tackle environmental challenges and make an impact that lasts.
