About Firley

Who the heck are we and what do we do? Find out at our blog post. In summary, we're a group of high schoolers that really wants to stop environmental problems. Firley builds AI tools to help people live more sustainably and understand the world around them. Our products, like EcoLens and MicroLens, make it easy to recycle, identify plants, and explore the microscopic world. We aim to inspire everyone to take small, meaningful steps toward a greener future.
